Draw an area of interest
Instructions
  • Move to a desired place. Use the mousewheel or add/ remove to change the zoom level.
  • Click on pentagon and start drawing an area.
  • To finish the area, click on the area's first point or click on the Finish button next to pentagon.
  • After finishing the area, it can still be edited using the draw icon. When edition is finished, click on Save or Cancel next to draw to save or rollback the changes.
  • The drawn area can be removed using the delete icon. After removal, click on Save or Cancel next to delete to save or rollback the changes.
